Types of Phishing Attacks

Email Phishing

Email phishing is one of the most common types of phishing attacks, where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations to steal sensitive information from individuals.

Smishing (SMS Phishing)

Smishing involves sending fraudulent SMS messages to trick users into revealing personal information.

Vishing (Voice Phishing)

Vishing occurs over phone calls, where attackers pretend to be legitimate entities and ask for sensitive data like credit card details.

Clone Phishing

In clone phishing, a legitimate email is copied and sent with malicious links or attachments, making it look like the original communication.

How to Protect Yourself from Phishing

  • Always verify email addresses and links before clicking.
  • Enable two-factor authentication on your accounts.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Never share personal information over unsecured channels.
